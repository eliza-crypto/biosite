About Me

My name is Eliza Tulod, and I am currently a web and graphic design student at El Camino High School. I was born and raised in the Philippines before moving to Oceanside, California, where I continue to explore my love for creativity and technology. Outside of school, I enjoy sewing, thrifting, playing Roblox, and sipping on chai or Thai iced tea. I also have a big appreciation for music, especially Radiohead, which inspires me in both subtle and creative ways.

In web design, I enjoy the process of combining creativity with technical skills. Designing layouts, experimenting with colors, and coding websites give me the opportunity to express ideas in a new way, while also learning skills that are useful for the future. I like how web design connects both art and problem-solving, which makes each project exciting and challenging.

Looking ahead, I hope to continue growing as both a designer and a student, whether that means pursuing web design, graphic design, or exploring other passions like biology.
